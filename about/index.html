Thinker, explorer, technologist

Hi! I use software to solve big and important business problems.

What is important, is not only what you do, but how you do it.

I choose my tools with care, based on the problem. Taking time to gain clarity as challenges come, and working out how best to proceed. The key to understanding, involves clear & effective communication, whether presented verbally, visually or written.

Most businesses suffer from major inefficiencies, wasting time and resources on processes, which do not benefit the business. I deeply care about removing these barriers, streamlining processes, and creating systems. Resulting in improvements in key areas such as performance, scalability, and security.

This gives your business more time to focus on growing, and time in which you could provide your clients with more value.

I build for a better tomorrow, not at the cost of maintainability.

I use this space to document thoughts, learnings, projects and my professional journey.

I’ve written it for future me, and it should also bring value to you.
